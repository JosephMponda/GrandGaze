## Safety Controls

- Role-based access control
- Authentication-protected access to all clinical views
- Audit trails for key actions
- Allergy and abnormal-result alerting
- Structured documentation for accountability

## Governance Principles

- Patient privacy and confidentiality
- Least-privilege access
- Traceable user actions
- Clear ownership of clinical and administrative workflows
- Support for teaching and research without compromising care

## Operational Safeguards

- Alerts for critical findings
- Separate workflows for emergency and routine care
- Session and navigation patterns that reduce error
- Data handling aligned with low-resource deployment realities
